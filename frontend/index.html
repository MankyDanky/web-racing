RACEZ.IO - Game Info

About the Game

RACEZ.IO is a multiplayer 3D racing game where you can race against friends across unique tracks. Create or join a party to play with others, or practice in a solo game.

Controls

  • W - Accelerate
  • S - Brake/Reverse
  • A - Steer Left
  • D - Steer Right
  • R - Reset Car (if stuck)
